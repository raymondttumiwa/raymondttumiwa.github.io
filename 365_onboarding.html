The 15-Minute Calibration: Reclaiming Your Life

Most of us wake up and immediately hand our attention over to a glass screen. Between the flood of urgent emails 📧 and the loud pings of social media, we aren't living—we’re just reacting. The 15-Minute Calibration is your daily invitation to hit the brakes and reconnect with the Creator of Life before the world demands your attention. Don't just start your day; redeem it through the word. ✨

Phase 1: Setup

  • ⏰ Set time: Choose dedicated time daily.
  • 📖 Open Blessings365: Read today's passage only.
  • 🙏 Pray: Start with Prayer.
