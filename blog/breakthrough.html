We all stand at a crossroads when it comes to our abilities. One path whispers that our talents are fixed, innate, and unchangeable. The other insists that growth is always possible through effort and the right process. I found myself squarely at this junction not long ago, feeling stuck.

For example, when I first tried to create a meaningful presentation, I kept thinking: "How do mentors get such DEEP insights?" I read the Bible every day, and it just felt flat. I realized that right there was a FIXED MINDSET.

The Ten Breakpoint of Breakthrough

1. Begin with Care

Breakthrough starts when your "why" shifts from self-improvement to serving others. Seeing friends in Shanghai hungry for truth created the engine for my growth.

2. Initiate Meaningful Discussion

"Stop menyendiri!" Isolation is the enemy of breakthrough. Talk to your cell group and mentors.

3. Choose Curiosity Over Condemnation

Instead of dismissing intense methods, ask "Why?" This curiosity led me to swap digital distractions for a physical Bible, deepening my focus.

4. Make Focus (Not Time)

My framework involves allocating Spiritual/Physical growth to the mornings and nights, while reserving afternoons for "Soul" growth, such as my research at SJTU.

5. ATM: Amati, Tiru, Modifikasi

Observe, Imitate, and then Modify. I used AI tools to adapt deep-study methods into my own workflow.

6. Cultivate Grit

Passion for the truth must be combined with perseverance that is constantly corrected.

7. Think the Unthinkable

Dare to connect dots in new ways, just as Paul did in Athens.
